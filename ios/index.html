  • Competing Applications
  • Corkz Wine Info - by Applied Ambiguities, LLC

    S: Low Cost, Simple Interface, Wine Reviews
    W: Not Cross Platform, Can not manually add items
    O: Pushing into Android/Blackberry/Windows
    T: Already has a market share where my application does not, Has been positively reviewed by Wine websites
  • Cadent wineCellar - by Cadent Computing Inc.

    S: Low Cost, Pre-loaded Wine List with multiple categories, Cloud syncing
    W: Non aesthitic interface, iPhone version is most advanced, Not Cross Platform
    O: Pushing into Android/Blackberry/Windows, Social Media Sharing
    T: Already has a market share where my application does not
  • Your Wines HD - by El Nudo Molesto

    S: Low Cost, More of a Journal than database (appealing to people not familiar with databases)
    W: Not Cross Platform
    O: Pushing into Android/Blackberry/Windows, Social Media, Possible Market sponsorship with Wineries
    T: Already has a market share where my application does not, Considered one of the best wine applications out there
